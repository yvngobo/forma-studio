FAQ
Frequently asked questions
How long does setup take? ▾
Most teams are up and running within 15 minutes. Connect your data sources, and Meridian automatically builds your first dashboard. No engineering resources required.
Can I import historical data? ▾
Yes. When you connect a data source like Stripe or your database, we automatically backfill historical data. Growth plans get 12 months, Enterprise gets unlimited history.
Is my data secure? ▾
Absolutely. Meridian is SOC 2 Type II certified, uses AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. We never sell your data. Enterprise customers can deploy in their own VPC.
What happens when I hit my MAU limit? ▾
We will notify you when you reach 80% of your limit. You can upgrade at any time. We never cut off your access or delete data — you just cannot add new tracked users until you upgrade.
Do you offer a free trial of Growth? ▾
Yes, every Growth plan starts with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. You get full access to all features during the trial period.
