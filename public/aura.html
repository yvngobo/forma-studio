Our Philosophy
Crafted for the
Considered Life

Aura Collective was founded in 2018 with a singular vision: to create clothing that transcends the noise of fast fashion. Every piece in our collection is designed to be worn, loved, and passed down.

We source only the finest materials from heritage mills across Italy, Scotland, and Japan. Our workshops employ artisans who have dedicated their lives to perfecting their craft.
