The Studio

About Nova

A small studio with
an outsized ambition.

We are a collective of designers, developers, and strategists who believe great work happens at the intersection of craft and curiosity. Founded in London in 2018, Nova has grown into a trusted partner for startups, scale-ups, and established brands alike.

Our approach is deliberately lean. Every project is led by a senior partner, ensuring the strategic thinking and creative vision you expect from a world-class studio without the layers of bureaucracy.
